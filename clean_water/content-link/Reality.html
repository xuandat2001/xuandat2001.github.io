Water - Esential platform of life.

Current situation of water resources.

Currently, you and your family still use clean, adequate and quality clean water? If so, congratulations that you are among the lucky people to have access to clean water in Vietnam, the rest there are many people who are using water that is not up to standard standards, even in many places where water is scarce. clean for daily living. However, many people who have access to clean water are wasting water resources.

Drought and flood in many places

Clean water sources are unevenly distributed, in some areas, plentiful water sources but in areas, there is a serious shortage of clean water. In some places during drought season, clean water is seriously lacking. Paradoxically, it is near the water source but there is no clean water to use ...

Groundwater sources are inefficient exploitation.

The sources of water we are using are long-standing groundwater, stored rainwater, treated tap water and bottled water ... But these water sources are at risk of pollution in some places. Toxic substances in agricultural production are gradually being absorbed into the ground, directly affecting groundwater. Groundwater is increasingly depleted due to over-exploitation, groundwater is not an endless source of water while the population is growing fast and the demand for water is increasing.

Domestic wastewater in the city.

Rain water has some people store it for use, but when they live near the places where the company or the industrial park discharges emissions, it will become acid rain, which is very dangerous to use.

Rain water near industrial. Other impacts of industrial zone.

Ponds, lakes, rivers, and streams in some places, the aquatic ecosystem is seriously destroyed by domestic wastewater, industrial parks or direct discharge plants without treatment. And some people discharge waste into water sources.

Discharged into water sources.

The sea water is poisoned by rubbish, oil spilling on the sea, discharging toxins into the sea (for example, the 2016 Fomosa factory discharge of toxins into the sea ....)
